Rasmus Højlund, the missing piece of the puzzle

Written byRaúl García Santana   Follow @raulgarciaa15
July 26, 2023
Share on Twitter Share on WhatsApp Share on Facebook Share via Email

Introduction

Rasmus Højlund is a 20-year-old Danish center forward who has been signed by Manchester United (from Atalanta) for around 70 million euros.


He is an agile player with good ball reception and a great shot.


Play Style

Counterpressing

  • Being a center forward with a lot of versatility and emphasis, he tends to corner his rivals intensely to cause the loss of the opposing team.
  • Said intensity is not only reflected in offensive actions but also in defensive plays.
  • His impetus to want to recover the ball is so explosive that it encourages the other teammates to do so.

Speed in Space

  • A player behind the defense is considered to be practically unstoppable.
  • His stride, drive, physical prowess, and intelligence allow him to score more easily than other forwards.
  • Here is a similar move, where he reads the uncheck and looks for the break through physicality and speed.
  • This time adjust the shot more so that it is practically impossible for the goalkeeper to save the ball.

Ball Protection

  • He is a player who is in charge of the "dirty work" of the team outside the area, picking up the balls and trying to get the most out of it.
  • Practically an out-of-class hungry for a goal.
  • When he receives with space, he is a brilliant striker since he seeks the best for the team with the game as a whole, he does not focus on finishing the play himself.
  • He knows how to move with his body in an exceptional way, in addition to making millimeter passes.
  • The ball is aerial? No problem, he lowers the ball even if he has a rival on top of him.
  • He is a shrewd striker with dazzling class who moves away from play to create space in his starting position.

Agility in the Box

  • Despite his size and physique, he is an unpredictable footballer who retains a lot of style.
  • Few people expect a precise heel shot inside the area made by a player who is 1.91 meters tall.

Relevant Data

  • He is one of the players from the 7 major leagues who has achieved the most:
  • Non-penalty goals per 90 (0.62).
  • Shots on target (57.3%).
  • Goals converted (26%).
  • Touches inside the box (5.58).

Role at Manchester United

In Ten Hag's team, Højlund would be the reference “nine”, who will delay his position somewhat to receive passes or lower balls and move them towards the band.


Conclusion

Despite the fact that in recent years Manchester United has been used to playing without a pure "9", the arrival of Højlund was necessary for all that it would bring to the club.
